Enjoy a peaceful getaway as you paddle and canoe among the rich flora and fauna of Lake Heritage. Drift across the calm waters, nourished by the Crocodile River. The Hi-Tec Water Course allows paddlers and canoeists to tailor their workouts. Delight in the rhythmic splash of your paddle and the breathtaking scenery, perfect for relaxation or fitness enthusiasts seeking a profound connection with nature.

Experience the tranquility of fly fishing at Cradle Moon, where serene waters and stunning landscapes create the perfect backdrop for this timeless sport. Whether you're a seasoned angler or a beginner, enjoy casting your line in the well-stocked waters, surrounded by the natural beauty of the Cradle Moon Conservancy. Join us for an unforgettable fly fishing adventure, just a short distance from the city.


Discover the rich flora and fauna of Cradle Moon Conservancy on a game drive with an experienced ranger. Covering the Zwartkop and Driefontein farms, including Ekudeni, Eduzani, Bell Gables, Riverview Spa, Glenburn Lodge, and Kloofzicht, the conservancy spans 1600 hectares. Encounter a variety of wildlife such as rhinos, elands, kudus, red hartebeests, black and blue wildebeests, zebras, duikers, impalas, springbucks, blesbucks, brown hyenas, jackals, and aardwolves.

At the heart of Cradle Moon Conservancy is the breathtaking 32-hectare Lake Heritage, home to a variety of fish, birds, and reptiles. Fed by the clear waters of the Crocodile River, it stands as Gauteng's top open water swimming destination, attracting swimmers, paddlers, and rowers for training. The Hi-Tec Water Course, marked by buoys, offers 500m, 1000m, and 1500m courses. Located centrally in the Cradle of Humankind, it is a popular training spot for MTB and road duathletes and triathletes, thanks to the surrounding trails and its accessible location.


Enjoy the excitement of trail running in the beautiful Cradle Moon Conservancy. Explore a variety of routes across 1600 hectares, suitable for beginners and experienced runners alike. Discover landmarks and wildlife as you run, fully immersing yourself in nature. Whether you prefer a relaxed jog or a thrilling adventure, our trails provide a unique way to connect with the outdoors and create memorable experiences.
