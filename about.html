All About Cradle Moon!

The History of Cradle Moon Lodge

Cradle Moon Lakeside Game Lodge is the revitalized version of the once-popular Heia Safari Lodge, originally established in the 1970s by the determined Franz Richter. Following Richter's passing, the lodge unfortunately experienced a period of decline.

Under the new ownership of Andy Dott and Kelvin van Niekerk, this stunning hidden treasure beneath the Zwartkop mountain is being rejuvenated to reach its full potential as the most sought-after eco-activity hospitality destination on the urban edge of Johannesburg. While significant renovations are underway, efforts are made to preserve the original character's unique charm.

Situated within the 1500-hectare Cradle Moon Conservancy, managed by a group of like-minded landowners, we offer a secure and pristine natural experience for visitors of all ages, from top athletes to those simply looking for fresh air. Our focus is on restoring the indigenous fauna and flora to revive the natural Highveld biodiversity.

Our 32-hectare pristine lake provides a full range of non-motorized water sports and is home to a family of hippos and numerous fish species for fly fishing enthusiasts. Additionally, we offer relaxing boat cruises on our 24-seater pontoon for those who want to explore the waters more extensively.
